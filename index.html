About HPlace

Where Tokyo Meets Bali

HPlace is Canggu's unique Japanese-inspired shisha lounge. We've blended the precision of Japanese cuisine with the relaxed atmosphere of a modern lounge — creating a space where you can enjoy premium hookah, fresh sushi, and craft cocktails in an industrial-chic setting.

Our space features exposed ductwork, lush greenery, and floor-to-ceiling bookshelves, creating an atmosphere that's both sophisticated and welcoming. Whether you're here for a casual lunch, evening shisha session, or late-night cocktails, HPlace delivers an experience unlike anywhere else in Bali.
